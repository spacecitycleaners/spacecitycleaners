Our Mission & Story

Our Mission

To deliver stellar cleaning services that create immaculate spaces for our clients. We combine cutting-edge scheduling technology with highly vetted, professional cleaners to ensure reliability, quality, and peace of mind for every booking, every time.

Our Founding Story

Space City Cleaners was founded in 2020 by a team of short-term rental hosts who were frustrated by the lack of reliable, high-quality cleaning services that understood the demands of the modern hospitality industry. We realized the market needed a solution that prioritized trust, efficiency, and communication.

Starting with a handful of properties in the Houston metro area, we built a platform designed to connect hosts with professional cleaners, ensuring every turnover was handled flawlessly. Today, we serve thousands of properties, maintaining our commitment to making cleaning the easiest part of property management.
